How to Request Your Data

May 5, 2023 patch 1

This document is unbound by The Waifu Legal Documents.

To request any Collected Data (any data that Virtual Waifu across Discord, Revolt or Crispy-Chat may have collected) collected by The Application (Virtual Waifu on either Discord, Revolt or Crispy-Chat), you can send an email to The Developers of The Application via the official support email: TallerThanShort@duck.com

Remember to state your User ID/User UUID and Platform, otherwise your request will be nullified (archived and not responded to). We keep Discord, Revolt and Crispy-Chat data collections separate, so you will need to file an individual request for each.

Beware!

TallerThanShort@duck.com is the only official support contact The Developers of The Application provide. If at any given point someone is attempting to impersonate us, please feel free to reach out with the following evidence:

  • Proof of impersonation

    • An image or video proving a User is attempting to impersonate The Developers of The Application.

    This can be:

    • Attempting to impersonate our email

      • Sending an email that is not TallerThanShort@duck.com, an email that displays TallerThanShort@duck.com but sends the email to another address once clicked (hyperlink physhing or markdown formatting in general).

    • Attempting to impersonate support

      • Requesting that you contact a User account on any platform (Discord, Revolt, Crispy-Chat or other), or requesting you go to a website outside the virtualwaifubot.com domain. We do not provide support outside of the email TallerThanShort@duck.com.

    • Impersonating The Official Website

      • Redirecting you to another website other than one on the virtualwaifubot.com domain.

    • Requesting you contact them for legal advice or legal help regarding The Application.

      • This can be a User requesting you contact them or any other User regarding any legal issue regarding The Application, rather than redirecting you to contact TallerThanShort@duck.com.

  • Platform Information

    • Tell us what platform they are performing the impersonation on. This can be any of the supported platforms (Revolt, Discord or Crispy-Chat), or another website entirely.

  • User or Server/Guild Information

    • Information regarding the User or Guild/Server where the impersonation takes place.

    We may request any of the following:

    • The User's Username, Tag and/or UUID
    • The Guild Invite Code, BounceBack UUID, and/or Owner
    • The Server ID, Name and/or Owner and/or the Owner's ID

FAQ:

Q: How often can I request my data?

A: You can request your data at any given time, without any limitations. We don't keep hardly any data, and furthermore, it's hashed by an automatic hashing algorithm.

Q: What are the weird lines under "infractions"?

A: These are Infraction ID's. They are hashed UUID's that The Application understands as your Infraction ID.

Q: Why is my ID/UUID innacurate?

A: We don't store any raw data. Your ID/UUID is hashed in an automatic hashing algorithm, along with all the data stored within.

Q: Why is the file blank?

A: We don't collect hardly any data. Only Users with Infractions (warnings) really have any Collected Data.

Q: How do I request my Guild's/Server's data?

A: You can request your Guild/Server data at any given time, without any limitations. You will most likely have a blank file, as we only store hashed ID's/UUID's of Guild's/Server's The Application is banned from by The Developers, along with a boolean (True/False status).
